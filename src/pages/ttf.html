THE TIDY FREAK

The creation of the brand The Tidy Freak, dedicated to professional organization and led by two passionate sisters, was a remarkable project. We faced the significant challenge of differentiating ourselves in a saturated market where most competitors opted for elegant and thin lettering. Our solution was a Bold typography, achieving a clean and distinctive design.
The CEOs of The Tidy Freak shared their vision of a fun and jovial yet refined brand. The colors were meticulously selected to convey peace, tranquility, and harmony, reflecting not only the services they offer but also how their clients feel in their homes.
