Welcome to my creative world. I am Valentina, a 27-year-old graphic designer, born in Caracas, Venezuela, and currently based in Atlanta.
From my childhood, I knew I was deeply connected to my creativity. However, it was at the age of 24 that I decided to listen to it and use it to pursue my passion for graphic design.
Every day, I immerse myself in new projects with a strong desire to learn, constantly seeking information that drives me to improve. My specialization is focused on visual identity design for entrepreneurial brands, as well as creating innovative visual content for social media.
I love the opportunity to transform concepts into innovative designs and am committed to providing visual solutions that highlight the uniqueness of each project. I am excited about the opportunity to continue creating impactful visual identities and invite you to explore how my vision can help elevate your brand.
