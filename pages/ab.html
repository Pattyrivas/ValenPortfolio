ALEJANDRA BERMUDEZ

ASESORA DE IMAGEN

Working on the development of the brand Alejandra Bermudez Image Consultant was an exciting and challenging project. The main goal was to create an identity that would allow both women and men to feel comfortable and confident with their appearance. The CEO had a clear vision: to represent the brand with her own name, use earthy tones to convey warmth, and reflect seriousness.
We chose a thin and elegant serif typography that fully encapsulates the brand's personality. Every element was carefully selected to achieve a cohesive and authentic visual representation. The final result not only reflects the CEO's vision but also establishes a meaningful connection between the brand and its audience.
